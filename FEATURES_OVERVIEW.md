# Education CRM - What You Can Do

This guide explains what users can do in the Education CRM application.

## 1) Sign In and Access Control

- Sign in with email and password.
- Stay logged in with session support.
- Use role-based access (Admin, Developer, Coordinator, Viewer, etc.).
- See only pages and actions allowed by your permissions.

## 2) Manage Student Inquiries

- View all inquiries in one place.
- Search, filter, and paginate inquiry lists.
- Create new inquiries quickly.
- Open inquiry details and update status.
- Track interactions and student preferences.

## 3) Manage Tasks and Follow-Ups

- Use a task dashboard for all work items.
- Track follow-ups due today or upcoming.
- Work in Kanban view (To Do, In Progress, Done).
- Use inbox-style task view for assigned items.

## 4) Plan with Calendar and Meetings

- View events in a monthly calendar.
- See meetings, follow-ups, and tasks by date.
- Open selected day details with full event list.
- Schedule and manage meetings with notes and location/link.

## 5) Run Campaigns and Outreach

### Campaigns
- Create and manage campaigns.
- Filter campaigns by status and performance fields.

### Promotion Codes
- Create/edit promotion codes.
- Track promoters and code performance.

### WhatsApp Campaigns
- Select recipients using filters (program, date, search, etc.).
- Compose messages with optional media.
- Save and reuse templates.
- Send in bulk and view sent/failed results.
- Review message history and recipient status.

### Email Campaigns
- Select recipients with valid emails.
- Compose subject and message with attachments.
- Send in bulk and monitor success/fail counts.
- View campaign history with preview and recipient statuses.

## 6) Social Media Post Approval Workflow

- Create posts with caption, media, and schedule fields.
- Submit posts for multi-step approval.
- Approvers can approve/reject with comments.
- Rejected posts can be revised and resubmitted.

## 7) Reports, Analytics, and Logs

- Use dashboard KPI cards and charts.
- Filter analytics by date range, user, and campaign.
- Create and manage weekly reports.
- View annual reports (admin-focused).
- Track activity logs and export data.

## 8) Data and Content Management

- Manage Programs and Levels.
- Manage Program Descriptions.
- Maintain Q&A/FAQ per program.
- Use Projects and Deals modules.
- Create and organize Notebooks and Notes.

## 9) Users, Roles, and Settings

- Manage system users.
- Create/edit roles and assign permissions.
- Update system and account settings (permission-based).

## 10) Notifications and Reminders

- Get in-app notifications with unread counts.
- Mark notifications as read (single or all).
- Use deep links from notifications.
- Enable browser push notifications (if supported).
- Receive unified reminders for meetings, tasks, and notes.

## 11) AI and Productivity Tools

### AI Assistant
- Open the AI Assistant page for help and content generation.
- Ask questions and get responses inside the CRM workflow.

### Mini Games
- Use the Mini Games section for short brain-training breaks.
- Play games like Snake, Memory, Math Quiz, Word Scramble, and Reaction Time.

## 12) Utilities

- Use Trash Bin to restore deleted inquiries/campaigns (with permission).
- Legacy routes are redirected to the latest pages where applicable.

## Who This App Is For

- Admissions and inquiry teams
- Campaign and outreach teams
- Coordinators and managers
- Admin users managing roles, settings, and reports

## Quick Outcome

With this application, your team can:

- Capture and manage leads
- Follow up on time
- Run WhatsApp and email campaigns
- Manage posts and approvals
- Track performance and reports
- Keep operations organized in one CRM
